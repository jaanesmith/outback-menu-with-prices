Outback Steakhouse Menu With Prices: What to Expect Before You Dine

Outback Steakhouse has built a strong reputation as a casual dining restaurant where bold flavors, hearty portions, and a relaxed atmosphere come together. Inspired by Australian themes and known for flame-grilled steaks, Outback offers a menu that appeals to families, steak lovers, and anyone looking for a satisfying sit-down meal. If you’re wondering what’s on the Outback Steakhouse menu and how much it typically costs, this guide breaks it all down in a simple and easy-to-understand way.

Steaks and Signature Favorites

Steaks are the heart of the Outback Steakhouse menu. Each steak is seasoned with the restaurant’s signature blend and cooked over an open flame to deliver rich, smoky flavor.

  • Center-Cut Sirloin prices usually range from $15 to $22, depending on portion size.
  • Ribeye Steak, known for its marbling and bold taste, typically costs around $33 to $41.
  • Victoria’s Filet Mignon is one of the most tender options on the menu, with prices generally falling between $28 and $39.
  • New York Strip offers a balance of flavor and texture, usually priced around $30.

For guests who want more variety, Outback also offers steak combinations that pair steak with grilled shrimp or other seafood options, creating a surf-and-turf style meal.

Chicken, Ribs, and Hearty Entrées

The menu goes far beyond steak, offering a wide range of comfort-style entrées.

  • Alice Springs Chicken typically costs $21 to $24 and is topped with bacon, mushrooms, cheese, and honey mustard sauce.
  • Grilled Chicken on the Barbie is a lighter option, usually priced around $16 to $18.
  • Chicken Tender Platter is a crispy favorite, often costing about $16 to $17.
  • Baby Back Ribs range from $22 to $30, depending on portion size.
  • Creamy Chicken and Shrimp Pasta is another filling option, usually priced near $22.

Side Dishes and Add-Ons

  • Aussie Fries generally cost $4 to $5.
  • Loaded Mashed Potatoes fall around $6 to $7.
  • Steakhouse Mac and Cheese is typically priced between $6 and $7.
  • Fresh Steamed Vegetables are a lighter option, usually around $4.
  • Sweet Potatoes with honey and brown sugar range from $4 to $5.

Kids Menu Options

  • Chicken Fingers usually cost $7 to $9.
  • Grilled Cheese is priced around $7 to $8.
  • Kids Cheeseburger ranges from $8 to $12.
  • Joey Sirloin typically costs around $10.
  • Ice Cream Sundaes are available for about $3.

Desserts and Sweet Treats

  • Chocolate Thunder from Down Under usually costs $10 to $11.
  • Carrot Cake costs approximately $9 to $11.
  • New York-Style Cheesecake ranges from $9 to $10.
  • Warm Cookie Skillet Desserts are priced between $8 and $9.

Drinks and Special Offers

Outback offers a full selection of soft drinks, cocktails, beer, and wine. Prices vary by location, and seasonal promotions or limited-time specials may be available through the restaurant’s app.

Things to Know Before Visiting

  • Prices may vary slightly by location and region.
  • Lunch portions and specials are often more affordable than dinner options.
  • Online ordering and takeout are available at most locations.
  • Special deals may be offered through the mobile app.

Final Thoughts

The Outback Steakhouse menu with prices offers something for nearly everyone, whether you’re in the mood for a perfectly grilled steak, comforting chicken dishes, family-friendly meals, or indulgent desserts. With fair pricing, generous portions, and consistent quality, Outback remains a popular choice for casual dining across the United States.

If you’re planning a visit, knowing the menu and price ranges ahead of time helps you order confidently and enjoy the experience even more.
